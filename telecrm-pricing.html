FAQ

Pricing questions, answered plainly.

Is there a refund policy?
Yes. Full refunds are offered within the first seven days for quarterly and annual plans, provided the customer participates in a feedback call. Under certain circumstances where promised features are not delivered, we may offer a pro-rated refund for the unused subscription period.
What is the pricing for WhatsApp Cloud API messaging?
There is a one-time setup charge of ₹1,999. There are no additional telecrm charges to use the WhatsApp automation features. You will still need to pay the official WhatsApp API messaging charges to Meta, which vary by message type and volume.
Are there any limits on leads or contacts?
No. Both the quarterly and annual plans give you unlimited contacts and leads — no caps, no per-lead charges. You can also access all integrations and all features at any team size.
Can I import my existing leads?
Yes. You can import leads from any Excel sheet — whether your data is in a spreadsheet or exported from another CRM. Map the columns to your telecrm fields with a few clicks and your existing data is live immediately.
Do callers need technical training to use telecrm?
No. telecrm is designed to be used without technical knowledge. Anyone who uses WhatsApp can use telecrm. Most teams are fully operational within a day of onboarding.
Will every call be recorded?
Yes. Every incoming and outgoing call (except personal SIM calls) is recorded automatically. Admins can access any recording at any time from their phone or computer — no manual action required by the rep.
