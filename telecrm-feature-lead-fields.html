Field Properties

Control how every field behaves.
Not just what it stores.

Each field can be configured with a set of properties that control its behaviour — how it appears, what it accepts, and how it participates in imports, templates, and quick-add flows.

Lock after create

Field cannot be edited once a lead is created. Ideal for source tracking, UUID fields, or any data that must remain immutable. Set it on creation — it stays that way forever.

Duplication & Uniqueness

One person. Multiple records.
All under control.

telecrm handles complex real-world scenarios — where the same contact has multiple active leads (e.g. 3 vehicles at the same dealership), and where you need a unique, customisable identifier for every lead record.

Lead UUID
TLC-2025-MH-RHS-00481

Every lead gets a UUID — a unique alphanumeric identifier of up to 256 characters.

UUID format is fully customisable per customer account — include prefixes, regions, rep codes, or any custom segments.

One-time setup only — once configured, every new lead automatically gets its UUID assigned on creation.

Ideal for managing multiple entries for the same person — e.g. a customer with 3 vehicles at the same dealership, each with its own UUID.
