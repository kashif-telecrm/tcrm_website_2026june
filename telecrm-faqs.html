Getting Started
What is telecrm and who is it built for?
telecrm is a Comprehensive Revenue Management Engine built for Indian sales teams. It fuses Lead Management, Call Management, WhatsApp, Automation, and Reporting into one mobile-first platform. It is built for businesses that sell over the phone and WhatsApp — real estate, education, healthcare, BFSI, automobile, travel, digital marketing, recruitment, services, manufacturing, start-ups, and more.
How long does it take to get started?
Most teams are up and running within a day. The setup is guided — you don't need a technical team or months of configuration. You can book a demo and our team will walk you through the setup for your specific business.
Is telecrm available on mobile?
Yes. telecrm is built mobile-first for Android. Reps can make calls, log activity, manage leads, send WhatsApp messages, check in at client locations with GPS, and view their pipeline — all from their phone. A web interface is also available for managers and desktop users.
Can I try telecrm before committing?
Yes. You can request a free demo and our team will show you exactly how telecrm works for your business — your industry, your team size, your lead sources. No commitment required.
What team sizes does telecrm work for?
telecrm works for teams of all sizes — from a 3-person start-up to enterprises with hundreds of reps across multiple cities and teams. The platform scales without re-implementation as you grow. See how it works for start-ups and enterprise teams.
Does telecrm save money compared to running multiple tools?
Yes — significantly. Businesses switching to telecrm from a stack of separate tools typically eliminate spend on standalone dialers, WhatsApp platforms, lead management tools, reporting dashboards and field tracking apps. telecrm consolidates all of these into one platform. See the full breakdown on the Cost Savings page.

Calls & Recording
Does telecrm support automatic call recording?
Yes. Every call made or received through telecrm is recorded and stored against the lead automatically. No manual action required from the rep. This requires a compatible Android device with a manufacturer dialer — not the Google Dialer.
Does call recording work on iPhones?
No. Auto call recording is not available on iPhones due to iOS restrictions. It is only available on compatible Android devices — specifically those with a manufacturer dialer and native auto-recording support, typically found in Indian brands and select Samsung models.
What is the difference between the manufacturer dialer and the Google Dialer?
The manufacturer dialer is the default phone app built by the device maker (e.g. Samsung's Phone app). The Google Dialer is a separate app pre-installed on some Android phones. The Google Dialer does not support auto call recording — so telecrm requires the manufacturer dialer to be the default.
Does telecrm log calls automatically?
Yes. Every inbound and outbound call is automatically logged against the relevant lead — duration, timestamp, recording and outcome — without the rep doing anything manually. Over 1 crore calls are logged on the platform every month.
Can managers listen to call recordings?
Yes. Managers can access and listen to recordings from the lead's activity timeline or the reporting dashboard. AI call summaries are also available — giving managers key insights from every call without listening to each recording in full.
What are calling campaigns and how do they work?
Calling campaigns let managers assign batches of leads to reps with defined follow-up sequences and call targets. Reps work through their assigned list systematically — making calls, logging outcomes and triggering next actions — without manually deciding who to call next. Useful for outbound prospecting, re-engagement drives and peak-season follow-up. See the Calling Campaigns page.

What is WhatsApp Chat Sync and how does it work?

Devices
Which Android phones work best with telecrm?
Recommended Samsung models include: M14, M34, F34, A05s, A12, A15, A14, and M30. These are tested and verified for full compatibility including auto call recording. See the full Recommended Devices page.
What are the minimum device specifications?
Minimum requirements: 4 GB RAM, 64 GB internal storage, Android 9 or above. The device must use a manufacturer dialer and support native auto call recording.
Why doesn't telecrm support the Google Dialer?
The Google Dialer does not provide access to call recordings at the system level. This is a Google platform restriction — not a telecrm limitation. Manufacturer dialers on Indian Android brands provide the system-level access telecrm needs to capture and store call recordings automatically.
Does telecrm work on tablets or desktops?
telecrm has a full web interface accessible from any browser on desktop or tablet. Managers typically use the web app for reporting and pipeline oversight, while reps use the Android app for calls and daily activity.
