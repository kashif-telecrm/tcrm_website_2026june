How telecrm compares

Built for India. Not adapted for it.

Most CRMs in India are global products built for Western sales cycles — then localised with a ₹ symbol and an IndiaMart integration. telecrm was built from the ground up for the way Indian sales teams actually operate: high call volumes, WhatsApp-first communication, JustDial and IndiaMart as primary lead sources, and owners who need visibility without complexity.

Calling is the core — not an add-on
1-click dialling, automatic recording, and call-linked lead updates built natively into every workflow.
Indian lead sources, natively integrated
IndiaMart, JustDial, Housing.com, 99acres — not through Zapier workarounds, but built-in direct connections.
Priced for Indian business reality
No enterprise pricing for basic features. Teams of 3 and teams of 300 both get the full platform.
