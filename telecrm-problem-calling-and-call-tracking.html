Problems Hub · Calling & Call Tracking

Make sales calls instantly.
Track every call automatically.

telecrm helps sales teams call leads faster, track every rep's activity, record every conversation, and turn incoming or missed calls into follow-ups — without manual dialling, spreadsheets or blind trust.

Where it breaks down

Six problems your team
is probably living with right now.

Problem 01
Manual dialling slows reps down
Reps copy numbers from sheets, WhatsApp and lead lists before every call. Manual dialling means delays, mistakes and constant app-switching — and calls made outside the CRM leave no trace for managers to see.
Problem 02
Fresh leads go cold before the first call
New leads sit uncalled while reps finish other work. Speed-to-lead depends on rep discipline, not an automatic workflow. By the time the first call happens, the buyer has already spoken to a competitor.
Problem 03
Incoming and missed calls slip through
Known leads call back and reps answer with zero context. Missed calls trigger no alert, no follow-up task. Unknown numbers never become new leads — warm inbound interest disappears without a trace.
Problem 04
No way to verify rep activity
Reps say they called — but there's no automatic proof. Call attempts, connected calls and missed follow-ups are not visible in one place. Daily updates are self-reported and impossible to verify without recordings.
Problem 05
Conversation quality stays a guess
Managers don't know what reps actually say on calls. No recordings or summaries to check pitch quality. Missed objections, wrong promises and weak follow-up scripts go uncorrected for weeks before patterns emerge.
Problem 06
Sales performance has no visibility
Hard to compare calls across reps. Connect rates, duration and calling hours aren't easy to track. Managers can't identify top performers — and weekly reviews rely on rep self-reporting rather than verified data.
How telecrm fixes it

Six fixes — one platform.

Every capability below is available on mobile and desktop, configured for your team in hours, and running the moment you go live.

1-click calling from the lead card
Call leads directly from telecrm without copying numbers from sheets, WhatsApp or personal phones. Reps move faster, avoid dialling mistakes and every call is automatically logged against the right lead from the first ring.
Incoming call screen-pop with context
Show lead details the moment a customer calls — name, source, stage and past activity. Reps answer with context instead of starting cold. Customers feel remembered. Conversion on inbound calls goes up.
Missed call follow-up automation
Never let warm inbound interest disappear silently. Missed calls are highlighted for follow-up and unknown numbers are automatically created as new leads — so every missed call becomes a follow-up task, not a lost opportunity.
Auto call logging with recordings
Capture every call against the right lead with time, status, duration and rep details. Managers get proof of activity without chasing the team for end-of-day updates. Recordings are stored and playable from the lead card.
AI call summaries
Review what was said on calls without listening to hours of recordings. AI summaries surface pitch quality, objections, promises and next steps in seconds — so managers coach in minutes, not after marathons of playback.
Live call performance reports
Track calls made, connected calls, calling hours and rep-wise performance through hourly, weekly and monthly reports. See who is working, who needs coaching and which hours produce the most connected calls — in real time.
Also worth knowing

Built for the way
Indian sales teams actually work.

Works with cloud telephony you already use
CallerDesk, Tata Tele, KnowLarity, MCube, TeleCMI — telecrm connects to all of them. You keep your number and your telephony provider; you just add full CRM context and automatic logging on top.
SIM card sync for teams without cloud telephony
Reps not using cloud telephony? telecrm syncs calls made from personal SIM cards directly into the CRM. Every call captured — zero disruption to how the team currently makes calls.
Progressive dialling for high-volume teams
For telecalling operations handling 200+ calls a day, progressive dialling keeps reps connected back-to-back. Connected call ratios go up; dead air between calls goes down; daily output per rep increases significantly.
Returning callers connect to the same rep
When a known lead calls back, telecrm routes them to the rep who last spoke with them. The buyer doesn't repeat their context. The rep picks up where they left off. Continuity that converts.
