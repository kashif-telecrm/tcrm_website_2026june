Common questions

Let's clear a few things up.

If yours isn't here, ask us on the demo call.

How does telecrm sync WhatsApp messages if reps use their personal numbers?
telecrm connects to your reps' WhatsApp numbers through the WhatsApp Business API. Once connected, every message they send or receive against a lead is automatically pulled into telecrm and logged on that lead's page. Reps still use WhatsApp normally — they don't change anything about how they work.
Are call recordings stored automatically or does the rep need to start them?
Automatic. Every outgoing and incoming call your reps make through telecrm's integrated dialer is recorded without any action required. The recording, duration, direction and AI summary are all logged against the lead page within seconds of the call ending. No manual steps, no missed recordings.
What happens to all the communication history if a rep leaves the team?
It stays in telecrm. Every call recording, every WhatsApp message and every email is stored against the lead — not the rep. When a new rep takes over, they see the full history from day one. Nothing walks out the door.
Can I search across all conversations to find what a rep said to a specific lead?
Yes. You can open any lead's page and see every call, WhatsApp thread, email and SMS in a single chronological timeline. You can also filter by channel, rep, date or keyword. If a conversation happened through telecrm, it's findable.
How long does it take to get the team set up and syncing?
Most teams are live within 48 hours. Our team handles the integrations, connects your WhatsApp numbers, imports existing data and trains your reps. You don't set up anything yourself beyond pointing us at your accounts.
