Minimum specifications

What to look for
in any Android device.

Not using a Samsung? telecrm works on any Android device that meets these minimum specifications.

4 GB
Minimum RAM
4 GB RAM ensures smooth multitasking between telecrm, the dialer, and WhatsApp without lag or crashes.
64 GB
Minimum Storage
64 GB internal storage gives enough room for the app, call recordings, and data — without running out of space.
Android 9+
OS Version
Android 9 or above is required for telecrm's background processes and notification features to work reliably.
Critical requirements

Two things that
really matter.

Beyond RAM and storage, these two requirements are non-negotiable for telecrm's call features to work correctly.

Manufacturer Dialer — Not Google Dialer
The device must use the manufacturer's built-in dialer — not the Google Dialer. Devices with Google Dialer pre-installed as the default (such as some stock Android phones) will not support auto call recording.
Auto Call Recording Support
The device must natively support auto call recording. This feature is typically only available on Indian brands and select Samsung models — it is not available on iPhones or most international Android variants.
