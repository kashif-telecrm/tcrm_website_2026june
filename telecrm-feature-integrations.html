Bulk & Manual Entry

Need flexibility beyond integrations?
No problem, we've got you covered.

telecrm gives you two handy ways to bring in leads manually — whether you're ingesting from a spreadsheet, uploading records in bulk, or adding a lead on the spot during a call or meeting.

CSV Upload
  • Upload any CSV — telecrm maps your columns to lead fields in seconds.
  • Migrate thousands of records in one shot.
  • Source tagging, duplicate detection, and instant assignment included.
Manual Lead Entry
  • Create a lead in seconds right from the telecrm dashboard.
  • Ideal for walk-in enquiries, referrals, or off-channel leads.
  • Fill in the details, assign it, and start working it immediately.
