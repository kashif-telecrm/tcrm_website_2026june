Before you request a demo

Questions we always get.

How many custom pipeline stages can I create?
telecrm supports unlimited custom stages. You can create, rename, reorder and colour-code them to match your exact sales process. Each stage can have its own colour and position, and reps move leads between stages manually as deals progress. The analytics update each time a rep moves a stage.
Can I track lost reasons at the rep level, not just overall?
Yes. The Lost Reasons report can be filtered by assignee, date range and lead source. You can see which rep is losing most deals to price, which is losing to competitor and which stage they typically lose at. This makes it possible to identify whether a loss pattern is a rep training issue or a product/pricing issue.
Does the pipeline update automatically or do reps have to log stage changes manually?
Reps update the stage manually when they move a deal forward — it's a single field change on the lead profile. You can also set up automation rules that change the stage based on specific events — for example, automatically moving a lead to "Demo Scheduled" when a meeting is booked, or to "Lost" after a set number of unsuccessful contact attempts.
Can I export pipeline reports for presentations or external reviews?
Yes. Every report in telecrm can be exported as a CSV file or downloaded as a chart image. The CSV export includes lead-level data — stage, assignee, date moved, lost reason — so you can do further analysis in Excel or build custom dashboards in BI tools like Metabase.
Can different teams have different pipeline stages?
Pipeline stages in telecrm are configured at the workspace level — one set of stages applies across the account. If you need different stage logic for different teams or products, this can be handled using lists, labels and custom fields to segment the pipeline view. Teams using distinct sales processes may benefit from separate workspaces.
