FAQ

Your questions, answered plainly.

How does telecrm personalise messages?
It merges each lead's own data — name, product interest, source, history — into your messages, so every contact reads as individually written.
Can I personalise at scale?
Yes — personalisation is driven by lead data and segments, so thousands of leads each get a relevant, one-to-one message without manual effort.
What data can I personalise with?
Any field on the lead record, including custom fields, plus interaction history like calls and messages.
Does personalisation stay accurate?
Yes — telecrm keeps every lead record current automatically, so your personalisation is always based on up-to-date information.
