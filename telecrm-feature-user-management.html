Team Structure

Your Teams. Your rules.
Total control how everything works.

telecrm gives you complete control to decide your work flow — how prospects, leads and customers data flows amongst teams — who can access what, what actions they can take, and what they can see. Set it up once and your custom telecrm setup runs exactly the way you want it to.

Workspaces

Organise your team in a single workspace or into multiple department-level workspaces — Pre-Sales, Sales, Post-Sales — whatever fits your business. Each workspace has its own interface, hierarchy and users; user access can span workspaces or be limited as required.

In multiple workspace instances, a record automatically flows from one workspace to the other. Example: a lead from the Sales workspace moves into Post-Sales once the sale is made. No manual handover. No delays.

Roles & Permissions

Give every rep exactly the access
they need — and nothing more.

Assign roles to every user in your team — Root, Admin, Manager, Caller, or Marketing. Each role comes with a precise set of permissions. Root permissions are fixed. All others can be tweaked by Root or Admin to suit your exact workflow.

Roles & user permissions

telecrm has five built-in roles — Root, Admin, Manager, Caller, and Marketing — each with a distinct permission set. Root permissions are immutable. Admin can be tweaked by Root (license visibility required). Managers can manage callers but cannot download leads. Callers work only on their own assigned leads. Marketing has automations and integrations by default. Except Root, all other permissions can be tweaked.

Managing Teams in Run-time

Keep your team running smoothly.
Even when things change on the fly.

Real sales teams change every day — reps go on leave, new members join, lists need updating. telecrm makes it easy to handle all of this without disrupting the pipeline.

Leave & agent availability

Mark a rep as on leave and telecrm automatically redistributes their leads for that day. When they return, their pipeline is restored — no manual reassignment, no leads left unattended.

Add team members manually

Add a new rep in seconds — name, email, role, and team. They get an invite link and are ready to work immediately. No IT involvement needed.

Bulk team upload

Onboarding a large team? Upload all your reps via a CSV — names, emails, roles, and teams assigned in one go. Your entire team is live in minutes.

Accountability

Every action. Every user.
Full visibility into what your team does.

Know exactly what's happening inside telecrm at all times — every edit, call, download attempt, and login is logged against the user who did it.

Activity logs & monitoring

Every action taken in telecrm — lead edits, calls logged, exports attempted, logins — is recorded against the user who did it. Managers and Admins get full visibility into team activity. Hold your team accountable with confidence.
