What flavor of CRM suits your needs?

Whether you rely on high-volume calling, inbound leads, field visits, WhatsApp conversations, or AI-driven pipelines — every business sells differently. telecrm is configured to match your specific approach. Select your business requisites to see exactly how telecrm is built for it.

AI CRM

If you want your CRM to do more than record — to learn, predict, and recommend — telecrm uses AI and ML to surface the leads most likely to close, the actions most likely to convert, and the risks most likely to cost you revenue.

Explore AI CRM

Analytical CRM

If you are making resource allocation, hiring, or process decisions without reliable data, telecrm gives you a live dashboard that makes the right answer obvious — without waiting for a report to be assembled.

Explore Analytical CRM

Calling CRM

If calling is your primary channel, telecrm is built for it. 1-click dialling, automatic recording, and full call logging — all inside the CRM, with zero manual entry required from your reps.

Explore Calling CRM

Collaborative CRM

If your sales, marketing, and support teams are working from different systems, telecrm gives all of them a single view of every customer — so no lead is contacted twice and no conversation is lost.

Explore Collaborative CRM

General CRM

If you need one platform that handles everything without weeks of setup, telecrm is ready in under 24 hours — leads, calls, pipeline, follow-ups, and reporting, all working from day one.

Explore General CRM

Inbound CRM

If your business generates leads from IndiaMart, JustDial, Facebook, Google, or your website, telecrm captures every one automatically and routes them to the right rep before a competitor even picks up the phone.

Explore Inbound CRM

Marketing CRM

If you spend on Meta or Google and cannot trace it to closed deals, telecrm closes that loop. CAPI integration feeds real conversion signals back to your ad platforms — so your spend targets buyers, not browsers.

Explore Marketing CRM

Mobile CRM

If your team sells on the move, telecrm gives every rep full CRM access on their phone — lead records, call logging, follow-up reminders, and pipeline updates without needing to be at a desk.

Explore Mobile CRM

Operations CRM

If your operation relies on manual steps, handoffs, and data entry between tools, telecrm replaces all of it with automated workflows that run your process in the background while your team focuses on selling.

Explore Operations CRM

Sales CRM

If your sales team needs a clear pipeline and full visibility over every deal at every stage, telecrm gives managers a live view of every rep's activity — without anyone needing to send a status update.

Explore Sales CRM

Strategic CRM

If your revenue comes from repeat business, upsells, and long-term relationships, telecrm tracks every customer's journey and triggers the right upsell or cross-sell at exactly the right moment.

Explore Strategic CRM

WhatsApp CRM

If your team closes deals over WhatsApp, telecrm brings every conversation into one place — logged against the lead, visible to the manager, and triggering the right follow-up automatically.

Explore WhatsApp CRM
