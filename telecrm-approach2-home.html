What telecrm delivers

Built to deliver outcomes, not just features.

telecrm is engineered around the outcomes Indian businesses actually need — across every role, every team, and every stage of the revenue journey.

Manage Leads & Contacts
Every lead captured, assigned, and tracked — from every source, automatically, with zero manual entry.
Make Outreach Effortless
Automated sequences reach every lead at exactly the right moment — no manual effort, no missed follow-ups.
Unify All Communication
Calls, WhatsApp, and messages — all in one place. No conversations lost across personal devices or scattered inboxes.
Customise Workflows
Build workflows that match your exact process — triggers, automations, and stage rules that run without anyone managing them.
Optimise Workforce
Full visibility into every rep's activity — calls made, leads contacted, deals progressed. Coach and improve from real data.
Boost Lead Generation
CAPI integration feeds real conversion signals back to Meta and Google — so your ad spend targets buyers, not just browsers.
Improve Agent Productivity
1-click dialer, auto-logging, and a clear action queue — agents spend their time selling, not on admin tasks.
Track Sales Pipeline
See the full pipeline in real time — every deal, every stage, every value. Know exactly where revenue is coming from next month.
View Holistic Customer Journey
Every touchpoint connected — from first enquiry to post-sale. Full context behind every customer relationship.
Track Business Performance
Hour-by-hour reports, leaderboards, and custom dashboards — always know the state of your business, in real time.
Enhance Support Experience
Every support query tracked, routed, and resolved — no ticket falling through, no customer left waiting.
Ease of Cross-Sell & Up-Sell
Existing customers automatically triggered for upsell and cross-sell at the right moment — no opportunity left on the table.
Real-Time Insights
Live data on every metric that matters — no waiting for reports, no decisions made on yesterday's numbers.
Leverage Automation
Set workflows once — lead assignment, welcome messages, follow-ups, stage updates — and let automation run your process.
Employ Personalisation
Every message, every follow-up, every campaign — personalised to the individual. Not one blast to everyone.
Deploy AI & ML
telecrm gets smarter with every interaction — AI and ML power recommendations, predictions, and optimisations automatically.

What is telecrm?
telecrm is a Comprehensive Revenue Management Engine built for Indian sales teams. It fuses Intelligent Lead Management, High-Velocity Call Management and Recording, Adaptive CRM, Hyper-Personalised Marketing Automation, and Robust Sales Funnel and Pipeline Management into one platform. Trusted by Mahindra, Domino's, Cox & Kings and 5,000+ businesses across India.
