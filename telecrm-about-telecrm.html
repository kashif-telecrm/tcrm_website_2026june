Our story

telecrm was built with a clear purpose — to give Indian sales teams a platform that works the way they work, with the power to scale the way they want to grow.

India sells differently. We built for that.
Indian sales teams run on calling and WhatsApp. They move fast, work across geographies, and close deals through relationships and follow-through. We saw an opportunity to build a CRM designed specifically for this reality — affordable, mobile-first, and built around the tools Indian sales teams actually use every day.
Simple to use. Powerful enough to scale.
telecrm was designed from the ground up for the Indian market — not adapted from a global product, not stripped down for affordability. Every feature was built around real workflows: calling campaigns, WhatsApp automation, lead stages, pipeline management, and reporting that gives managers actual visibility. Easy enough for a small team. Powerful enough for an enterprise.
From a small team to 5,000+ businesses.
What started as a focused team has grown into a platform trusted by 5,000+ enterprises across India — spanning real estate, education, healthcare, finance, automobiles, and beyond. 100+ people now build, support, and grow telecrm every day. The platform keeps getting more powerful. The mission stays the same: help every Indian sales team sell better.
The best sales teams win on relationships, speed, and follow-through. telecrm is built to make all three effortless.
telecrm is built for the speed, scale, and relationships that define Indian sales. Simple enough that any rep can be productive on day one. Powerful enough that enterprises run their entire revenue operation on it.

That balance — ease and power — is the design principle behind every decision we make.
