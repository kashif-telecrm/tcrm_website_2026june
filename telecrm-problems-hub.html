FAQ

Your questions, answered plainly.

What is the Problems Hub?
It's a starting point organised by problem rather than feature — pick the gap that sounds like your team's, and it takes you to a page showing exactly how telecrm closes it.
Which problem page should I start with?
Start with whatever costs you most right now — scattered leads, untracked calls, missed follow-ups, or no visibility. Each page stands on its own.
Do I have to fix all of these at once?
No. Teams usually start with one or two problems and expand. telecrm is one platform, so solving one problem sets up the others.
How is this different from the Solutions Hub?
The Solutions Hub groups telecrm by CRM type, team and industry; the Problems Hub groups it by the specific revenue leaks it fixes.
