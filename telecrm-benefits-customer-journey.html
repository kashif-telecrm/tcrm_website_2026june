Before you request a demo

Questions we always get.

What exactly gets logged on the activity history?
Every call — connected or missed, with duration and the rep who made it. Every WhatsApp message sent through telecrm. Notes added manually. Stage changes. Field edits. Lead assignments and reassignments. Calls and WhatsApp messages are logged automatically — the rep doesn't need to do anything. Notes and stage changes are logged when the rep makes them.
Can a new rep see everything that happened before they were assigned the lead?
Yes. The activity history is attached to the lead, not the rep. When a lead is reassigned, the new rep opens the profile and sees the complete history — every call, every WhatsApp, every note — from all previous reps. There is no separate handoff process required. The context transfers automatically.
Can managers see the activity history for all leads, or only their own?
Managers and admin roles can view the activity history on any lead in the workspace. The Team filter on the activity history lets you filter by rep — so a manager reviewing a specific lead can see only what one rep did, or see everything across all reps. Permissions are configurable by role.
Does the history include WhatsApp messages sent from outside the CRM?
telecrm logs WhatsApp messages sent through the platform — either via the 1-click WhatsApp button inside the lead, or via the WhatsApp Cloud API integration. Messages sent directly from a rep's personal WhatsApp are not captured unless the rep's WhatsApp is synced into telecrm via the chat sync feature.
How far back does the activity history go?
The activity history is retained for the lifetime of the lead in the workspace. There is no automatic expiry or archiving. A lead created 3 years ago will still show every interaction from that point forward. The Time filter lets you narrow the view to a specific date range if the history is long.
