The automobile sales challenge

Automotive leads are expensive to generate
and easy to lose to slow follow-up.

A car buyer submitting an enquiry on CarDekho is simultaneously visible on three other portals. The dealership that responds first — not the one with the best price or the most features — wins the test drive booking. And the test drive is what wins the sale.

Most automotive dealerships lose leads not to competitors but to their own process gaps. Leads arrive across multiple channels, get noted in different registers, and are assigned to whichever sales executive happens to be at the desk when someone checks. Without a system that captures every lead instantly, deduplicates across sources and assigns immediately, the most motivated buyers are the ones most likely to go elsewhere — because they submitted enquiries quickly and expected fast responses.

What telecrm solves for automobile
telecrm gives automobile businesses a single pipeline for every lead from every source — deduplicated, assigned instantly, tracked through test drive to booking and delivery — so no lead is lost to a process gap and no manager is operating blind on their showroom floor.
What automobile teams need from a CRM

Five things that determine whether an enquiry
becomes a test drive — and a test drive a sale.

Automotive CRM requirements are defined by the specifics of the vehicle purchase journey — multi-source leads, a mandatory test drive stage, a multi-week decision cycle and a field sales component for OEM-level dealer relationship management.

1
Instant multi-source lead capture without duplication
CarDekho, Cars24, dealer website, Facebook Lead Ads, Google Ads and direct phone calls all generate automotive leads simultaneously. The same buyer appears across multiple sources within minutes of their first search. Without automatic capture and deduplication, that buyer is assigned to three different sales executives who call within minutes of each other — creating confusion and wasting rep time on a single prospect.
2
Test drive as a managed pipeline stage
The test drive is the conversion event in automotive sales. A lead that has completed a test drive converts at a dramatically higher rate than one that has not. A CRM for automobile businesses needs to treat test drive scheduling and completion as a formal pipeline stage — with dedicated follow-up rules, confirmation messages, GPS-verified completion logging and outcome notes. A test drive that is not recorded is a conversion event that cannot be analysed or replicated.
3
Full pipeline from enquiry to delivery
Automotive sales involves more stages than most industries — enquiry, first contact, test drive, model selection, finance application, booking, PDI, registration, delivery. Each stage has a distinct owner and a distinct follow-up requirement. A CRM that only tracks the first two or three stages leaves the majority of the customer journey unmanaged and unvisible to the sales manager.
4
Dealer and channel partner pipeline visibility for OEMs
OEMs and national distributors managing dealer networks need to see pipeline health across every dealer — total enquiries, test drive rates, booking conversion and delivery timelines — without requiring dealers to manually submit reports. Dealer performance visibility that depends on dealer reporting is always incomplete and always delayed.
5
Field sales executive tracking for OEM relationship management
OEM field sales executives visiting dealerships, service centres and channel partners need GPS-verified check-ins, visit logs and real-time field activity dashboards. Managers overseeing a territory of 50 dealerships cannot rely on verbal updates from field reps — they need verified data on which dealers are being visited, how often, and what is being discussed at each visit.
How telecrm works for automobile

From first enquiry to delivery —
every stage tracked across every channel.

telecrm is used by automotive dealerships, OEM field sales teams and channel partner networks across India. Mahindra FES and Ideal Dealers are among the automotive businesses that have used telecrm to manage sales operations at scale.

Multi-source lead capture
Every lead from CarDekho, Cars24, dealer website, Facebook and Google flows into telecrm automatically. No manual entry, no channel gaps — every enquiry is in the system and with a sales executive within seconds of submission.
Automatic deduplication
When the same buyer appears across multiple portals, telecrm identifies and consolidates into a single lead record before assignment. One buyer, one sales executive, one conversation — not three executives calling the same buyer simultaneously.
Test drive pipeline stage
Test drive scheduling, confirmation, completion and outcome are tracked as formal pipeline stages. GPS check-in verifies the test drive happened, structured post-drive notes capture the buyer's response and automated follow-up triggers immediately after the drive — when buyer intent is at its highest.
Full enquiry-to-delivery pipeline
Configure all stages of your automotive sales process — enquiry, first call, test drive, model selection, finance application, booking, PDI, registration, delivery. Every stage is tracked with timestamps, responsible executive and follow-up status — visible to the showroom manager in real time.
WhatsApp follow-up automation
Automated WhatsApp messages send at every stage — instant acknowledgement on enquiry, brochure and model information, test drive confirmation, post-drive follow-up, booking congratulations, delivery update. Buyers receive a consistent experience regardless of which executive handles them.
1-click calling with auto-logging
Sales executives call buyers directly from the lead record. Every call is recorded and logged automatically — duration, recording and outcome — so showroom managers can review call quality and track response times without requesting manual reports from the team.
Dealer network management
OEMs and distributors managing dealer networks see pipeline health across every dealer in one consolidated view — total enquiries, test drive rates, booking conversion, delivery timelines and executive performance — without requiring dealers to submit manual reports.
Field sales GPS tracking
OEM field executives visiting dealerships check in with GPS verification and photo capture. Territory managers see live field activity — which dealers have been visited, how often and what was discussed — without depending on verbal updates from field reps.
Showroom manager dashboards
Showroom managers see enquiries by source, sales executive performance, test drive conversion rates, booking pipeline value and delivery timelines in real time — without waiting for EOD reports or asking the team individually for updates.
Post-sale service & insurance pipelines
After delivery, the dealership relationship continues — vehicle insurance at the point of sale, first service reminders at 1,000km and 5,000km, annual service scheduling, insurance renewal reminders 45 days before expiry and accessory upsell outreach. telecrm manages all of these as automated post-sale sequences so the dealership retains the customer relationship beyond the sale itself.
Automobile business types

telecrm works for every type of
automobile business in India.

The sales process differs between a passenger car dealership, a two-wheeler showroom and an OEM field team — but the core need is the same: every lead captured, every test drive tracked and every executive accountable.

Passenger Car Dealerships
Multi-source lead to delivery
Car dealerships managing leads from portals, digital ads and walk-ins simultaneously need a single pipeline that captures and deduplicates every source, tracks test drives as formal stages and gives showroom managers real-time visibility into every executive's pipeline and activity.
Two-Wheeler Showrooms
High-volume fast-cycle sales
Two-wheeler showrooms handling high daily enquiry volumes need fast lead assignment, quick follow-up automation and stage tracking that moves at the speed of a shorter decision cycle — with the same pipeline visibility for managers as a four-wheeler operation.
Commercial Vehicle Dealers
B2B fleet and corporate sales
Commercial vehicle dealers managing fleet and corporate buyer enquiries need multi-stakeholder deal tracking, longer decision cycle management and post-delivery service relationship pipelines — distinct from the retail consumer purchase process.
OEMs
Dealer network and field team management
OEMs managing dealer networks and field sales executives need consolidated dealer pipeline visibility, GPS-verified field visit tracking and the ability to push leads centrally to specific dealers — without requiring every dealer to use a separate system.
EV Dealerships
Education-led sales process
EV dealerships selling to buyers unfamiliar with electric vehicle ownership need a longer nurturing pipeline — multiple touchpoints explaining charging infrastructure, range, cost of ownership and incentives — before the test drive and booking stages that close the sale.
Used Vehicle Platforms
Buyer and seller pipeline management
Used vehicle businesses managing both buyer enquiries and seller acquisition need separate pipelines for each side of the marketplace — with the lead capture, follow-up automation and executive accountability that applies to both buyer conversion and seller onboarding.
Post-Sale Services
Insurance, service and renewal management
Dealerships selling vehicle insurance at point of delivery, managing service reminders at 1,000km and 5,000km milestones, scheduling annual service appointments and sending insurance renewal reminders — all automated through telecrm so no post-sale revenue opportunity is missed.
