Before you request a demo

Questions we always get.

Can the WhatsApp chatbot handle industry-specific queries?
Yes. The chatbot is configured to your business — you define the questions it handles, the responses it gives and the buttons it shows. A real estate business would set it up differently from an insurance company or an EdTech platform. It responds to whatever scenarios you train it for, and escalates to a human agent when the query falls outside its scope.
What if a customer's query is too complex for the chatbot?
The chatbot includes a "Talk to an agent" option that routes the customer to a live support rep. When the handoff happens, the agent sees the full conversation the customer had with the bot — so the customer doesn't have to repeat themselves. The chatbot handles volume; agents handle complexity.
How accurate are the AI call summaries?
AI call summarisation captures the key issue, what was discussed and the suggested next step from the recorded call. Agents can edit the summary if anything needs adjustment. The recording is also available for playback, so managers can verify summaries and use them for quality control or agent training.
Can I schedule messages for specific customer segments only?
Yes. Scheduled messages use the same filter logic as the rest of telecrm — status, industry, lost reason, creation date, WhatsApp opt-out and any custom field. You define exactly which customers receive each message. A post-resolution follow-up only goes to customers whose case was closed in the last 48 hours, not your entire database.
Do call reports update automatically or require manual input?
Call reports update automatically as calls happen. No agent needs to log anything — connected calls, missed calls, duration and agent attribution are all captured by the system. Managers can view reports at any time and export to CSV for external presentations or reviews.
