Problems Hub · Lead Management

Excel can't capture your leads.
Sheets can't assign them.

Two failures inside lead management — how leads get captured, and how they get assigned — silently cost most teams 15% of monthly ad spend before a rep makes the first call.

Where it breaks down

Six problems your team
is probably living with right now.

Problem 01
Lead silos across 5+ sources
Leads land across IndiaMart, Google Ads, Facebook, WhatsApp and your website — each with its own login and format. Managers lose 1.5+ hours a day switching between portals just to compile a single lead list.
Problem 02
Lead leakage at every handoff
Manual copy-paste from source to Excel to WhatsApp drops leads at every step. WhatsApp enquiries get buried under personal chats. The leak stays invisible until a lead calls back asking why no one reached out.
Problem 03
Manual entry errors compound
Phone numbers get mistyped, names misspelled, fields left blank. Enquiries marked 'will do later' get forgotten. Across 1,000 leads a month, roughly 15% carry errors that affect every downstream action.
Problem 04
Same lead, three reps calling
A buyer enquires through three sources in a week. Without auto-dedupe, your CRM creates three records and assigns three reps. The buyer gets three calls from the same company in an hour and loses confidence.
Problem 05
Round-robin assignment ignores fit
Naive round-robin routing ignores skill, language, geography and past performance. Premium leads land with trainees who can't close them. Your best closers handle low-value cold leads while hot ones go cold.
Problem 06
Re-assignment falls through cracks
A rep goes on leave and 40 open leads sit untouched. No SLA timer, no auto re-assignment, no escalation when leads cool. By the time someone reassigns manually, half the leads have already booked elsewhere.
How telecrm fixes it

Six fixes — one platform.

Every capability below is available on mobile and desktop, configured for your team in hours, and running the moment you go live.

Unified lead inbox
Pull leads from IndiaMart, JustDial, Facebook, Google Ads, your website, WhatsApp and missed calls — into one unified inbox. Every source feeds the same pipeline. No more portal-hopping, no more morning consolidation.
Zero-leakage capture
Every enquiry is captured the moment it arrives, with a complete record of source, timestamp, channel and status. Nothing gets dropped, nothing gets buried — capture is the system's job, not the rep's memory.
Auto-populated lead fields
Lead details flow straight from the source into a structured record — phone, name, source, enquiry context — all populated automatically. No retyping, no format mismatches, no blank fields to chase.
Automatic deduplication
telecrm cross-matches every new lead by phone, email and source against your existing database. Duplicates merge into the original lead's history — so three portal enquiries from the same buyer become one record with one owner.
Smart assignment rules
The second a lead is captured, telecrm's assignment rules push it to the right rep — in under five seconds. Set rules by source, location, language, expertise and past performance. Premium leads go to senior closers. Always.
SLA-based re-assignment
Set SLA timers per stage. When a lead sits untouched beyond the threshold — rep on leave, rep overloaded, rep underperforming — telecrm re-assigns automatically. No manual intervention. No leads going cold unnoticed.

Does it prevent duplicate leads?
