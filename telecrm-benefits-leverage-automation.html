FAQ

Your questions, answered plainly.

What can telecrm automate?
Lead assignment, follow-ups, reminders, WhatsApp messages, and stage-based actions — anything repetitive in your sales process, triggered automatically by events or time.
Do I need a developer to set it up?
No. Automations are built with no-code rules, so you can set them up and change them yourself without technical help.
Will automated follow-ups feel impersonal?
They're sent on a reliable cadence but can be personalised per lead, so each contact feels handled while none get forgotten.
What happens to leads no one touches?
telecrm automatically re-assigns idle leads before they go cold, so nothing sits unworked.
