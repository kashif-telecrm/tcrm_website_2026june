Before you request a demo

Questions we always get.

Does telecrm work with Facebook Lead Ads specifically — not just the Facebook page?
Yes. telecrm integrates directly with Facebook Lead Ads — the native ad format that lets users submit a form without leaving the Facebook app. Leads captured via Facebook Lead Ads sync into telecrm in real time, with the full form data including name, phone, email and any custom questions you've included. An automated WhatsApp welcome message can be triggered the moment the lead submits.
Can I track leads down to the specific ad and campaign, not just the platform?
Yes. When leads come in from Google Ads via your website, the UTM parameters — campaign name, ad group, keyword — are passed directly into telecrm and stored against the lead. The "Source of Lead" field shows the exact campaign, so you can filter your lead list by campaign and see exactly which Google Ads campaigns are generating convertible leads versus noise.
How does duplicate detection work when the same person comes from two sources?
telecrm matches leads on phone number. If a number already exists in the system, the incoming lead is flagged as a duplicate. You can configure whether duplicates are merged into the existing profile or held separately for review. All source information is retained — so you can see that the same lead came from both Facebook and IndiaMart, which is useful attribution data.
What happens if my internet goes down — do I lose leads during the downtime?
No. Lead capture integrations run server-side on telecrm's infrastructure — not on your local network. Whether your office has connectivity or not, leads from Facebook, IndiaMart, your website and other connected platforms continue to flow into telecrm uninterrupted. When connectivity is restored, everything is already there.
We use a custom website form — can that integrate with telecrm?
Yes, via the Website/API integration. Any form on your website — built on WordPress, React, custom HTML or any other stack — can push lead data into telecrm using a simple REST API call. Your developer adds a single API call to the form's submit handler and leads start flowing immediately. telecrm also supports Google Tag Manager for no-code form tracking.
