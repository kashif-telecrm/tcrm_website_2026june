What telecrm can do

Useful features that power
business benefits.

telecrm is built for the reality of Indian sales — high call volumes, WhatsApp-first communication, distributed teams, and the need for real-time visibility at every level. Here is everything the platform does.

Trusted by Mahindra Domino's Cox & Kings Housing.com JioMart UpGrad and 5,000+ Indian businesses
Feature categories

Feature-rich, built deep. Not just wide.

Every feature in telecrm is built to the depth your business needs — not surface-level toggles. Select a category to see exactly what is included.

Integrations
  • Zero manual import. Every lead source — IndiaMart, JustDial, Facebook, Google, Housing.com — feeds directly into telecrm automatically.
  • Leads created in seconds. Every integration triggers instant lead creation — no lag, no batch imports, no window where a hot enquiry goes unassigned.
  • Connect almost any source. telecrm's custom API lets leads flow in from virtually any platform, portal, or proprietary system your business runs on.
Explore Integrations
Lead Fields
  • Built around how you sell. Create custom fields — text, numbers, dates, dropdowns, file uploads — so your CRM matches your process exactly.
  • Auto-mapped from every source. Fields from every lead source map to your telecrm structure automatically, no manual configuration required.
  • Role-level visibility control. Field-level permissions ensure each rep sees only what is relevant — no clutter, no sensitive data exposed.
Explore Lead Fields
Lead Management & Reporting
  • Every lead, every source. IndiaMart, JustDial, Facebook, Google, WhatsApp — all leads captured, deduplicated, and auto-assigned instantly.
  • Complete timeline, zero manual entry. Every call, message, and stage change logged automatically — full history from first touch to closed deal.
  • Real-time performance visibility. Live reports on conversion rates, response time, and team performance — always current, never assembled manually.
Explore Lead Management & Reporting
Call Management
  • Every call captured. 1-click calling with automatic recording — every conversation searchable and playable in 30 seconds.
  • Live team visibility. Real-time dashboards show who is calling, who is idle, and which leads have not been contacted.
  • No lead goes cold. Missed call alerts, call duration tracking, and IVR routing ensure every enquiry is handled automatically.
Explore Call Management
Calling Campaign Management
  • Structured, targeted outreach. Create calling campaigns from any lead list — assign reps, set targets, track progress in real time.
  • Full campaign accountability. Every campaign call is recorded, logged, and attributed — conversion rates measured per rep, per day.
  • Built for high-volume calling. Run re-engagement blitzes and follow-up campaigns at scale with complete visibility at every step.
Explore Calling Campaign Management
WhatsApp
  • Instant, automated outreach. Messages triggered by lead stage or time — every lead acknowledged instantly, every follow-up on schedule.
  • Unified conversation history. WhatsApp conversations logged in the lead timeline alongside calls — one complete view of every interaction.
  • Precision broadcast campaigns. Send personalised template messages to segmented lists at scale — the right message, the right leads.
Explore WhatsApp
Lead Stages
  • Your process, your pipeline. Define your own stages from first contact to closed won — drag-and-drop, fully controlled by your team.
  • Enforced data discipline. Required fields per stage mean reps cannot advance a deal without the information your business needs.
  • No deal left stalled. SLA timers escalate uncontacted leads automatically — no opportunity sits forgotten in a stage.
Explore Lead Stages
Reporting
  • Always-on leadership visibility. Hour-by-hour reports and leaderboards ranked by calls made and deals closed — always live, never delayed.
  • Pinpoint where revenue is lost. Funnel analysis by stage and source reveals exactly where leads drop off and which channels perform best.
  • Export-ready, no assembly required. Custom date range filters and exportable reports — no analyst needed, no data to gather manually.
Explore Reporting
Automation
  • Smarter ad spend. Meta CAPI sends real conversion signals back to your ad platforms so campaigns target buyers, not just clicks.
  • Your process runs itself. Trigger WhatsApp messages, reminders, and stage updates automatically based on behaviour or time elapsed.
  • Connects across your stack. Webhooks sync data downstream and escalate stalled leads — set once, runs continuously across your business.
Explore
User Management
  • Role-based access at every level. Reps see their leads, managers see their team, owners see everything — configured in minutes, no IT needed.
  • Granular visibility controls. Field, stage, and report-level permissions keep sensitive data protected and each role distraction-free.
  • Instant onboarding and restructuring. Add or reconfigure users in real time — onboard a new rep or restructure the team without downtime.
Explore User Management
List and Labels
  • Organised lead management. Create named lists for campaigns, territories, or any grouping — filter and act on any list in seconds.
  • Instant visual identification. Colour-coded labels show lead status at a glance — hot, visited, negotiating — without opening a record.
  • Precision targeting. Combine lists and labels to create exact segments for campaigns, broadcasts, or targeted follow-up.
Explore List and Labels
