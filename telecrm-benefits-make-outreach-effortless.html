The problem worth fixing

Your reps aren't lazy.
They're buried in busywork.

Most of a sales day isn't selling. It's dialling numbers, copying messages, remembering who to text next and trying not to forget the leads from last week. Here's what that looks like up close.

01
Dialling numbers off a sheet
Type the number. Wait. Dial again. Wait. By the time a connect happens, three minutes are gone. Multiply that by 80 calls a day and you've lost half the shift.
02
Saving every contact just to WhatsApp
Open contacts. Save number. Open WhatsApp. Find them. Send the message. Five steps for one ping. Your phone has 3,000 unsorted contacts and your message templates live in a Word file.
03
"I'll follow up tomorrow" never happens
Tomorrow becomes Friday. Friday becomes next month. By then the lead has moved on. Without a system, follow-up depends on memory and willpower. Both run out.
04
Same message, hand-typed 50 times a day
"Hi, this is Priya from XYZ, just following up on your enquiry…" Copy. Paste. Tweak the name. Repeat for every lead. The fastest reps still spend two hours a day on this.

Common questions

Things people ask
before getting started.

If you have a question that isn't here, just ask us on the demo call.

What is sales outreach automation?
Sales outreach automation is when your CRM handles the repeat work of contacting leads — calls, WhatsApp messages, reminders and follow-ups — so your reps spend their time on the actual conversations instead of remembering who to call next. telecrm runs your entire outreach motion automatically based on rules you set once.
How does telecrm's 1-click dialler work?
telecrm's 1-click dialler lets your reps make calls straight from a lead's profile in the CRM, with no number dialling and no copy-paste. It auto-logs every call, records the conversation, tracks call duration and shows the next lead automatically. For high-volume teams it also supports progressive and predictive dialling.
Can I send WhatsApp messages without saving each lead's number?
Yes. telecrm sends WhatsApp messages to any lead with a single click. No number-saving needed. You can use templates, send broadcasts to lead lists, set up automated drip sequences and run a 24x7 chatbot — all using the official WhatsApp Business API, so your account stays safe.
What kinds of follow-up reminders does telecrm support?
telecrm sends reminders for missed calls, scheduled callbacks, meetings, lead inactivity and recurring dates like birthdays, anniversaries, EMI dues, SIP dates and insurance renewals. You can also build custom reminders for any field in your pipeline.
How do automated follow-up sequences work?
You design a sequence once — for example, Day 1 welcome call, Day 2 WhatsApp, Day 5 reminder, Day 7 follow-up call. Every new lead that matches the trigger automatically goes through the sequence. Your reps just take the calls and reply when needed; everything else happens on its own.
Is telecrm built for high-volume Indian sales teams?
Yes. telecrm is built for the way Indian businesses sell — heavy phone outreach, deep WhatsApp usage and tight follow-up cycles. It supports cloud telephony partners like CallerDesk, Tata Tele, TeleCMI and Knowlarity, plus the official WhatsApp Business API. 5,000+ Indian teams use telecrm today, including Mahindra, BYJU'S and Housing.com.
