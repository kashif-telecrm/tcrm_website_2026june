Before you request a demo

Questions we always get.

Does the leaderboard update automatically or does someone have to trigger it?
Fully automatic. Every call made through telecrm's integrated dialer updates the leaderboard the moment it ends — no input from the rep, no refresh needed on your end. The number you see is accurate to the last call. Filter by Calls, Talk Time or Revenue and switch between Day, Week, Month and Year instantly.
Do reps know when their calls are being recorded?
Recording is on by default for all calls through the telecrm dialer — your reps are aware that calls on the platform are recorded. The recordings are stored against each lead's profile and are accessible to managers immediately after the call ends, along with the AI-generated summary.
What happens to leads when a rep is marked absent? Does it redistribute everything?
When you mark a rep absent, telecrm redistributes their open, uncontacted leads to active team members based on your configured assignment rules. Leads that are already in conversation are not moved — only those not yet reached. When the rep is marked active again, new leads resume flowing to them.
Can I track performance at team level, not just individual rep level?
Yes. You can filter the leaderboard and reports by team, by role or by individual rep. If you manage multiple sub-teams under one workspace, each team's performance is visible separately. Custom dashboards let you configure the specific KPIs that matter for each team or manager.
How far back do call recordings go? Can I access calls from three months ago?
All call recordings are stored against the lead profile — not against the rep — and remain accessible for as long as the lead exists in your account. You can find any call from any rep on any lead, at any point in time. Use it for coaching reviews, dispute resolution or onboarding new reps with examples of what good looks like.
