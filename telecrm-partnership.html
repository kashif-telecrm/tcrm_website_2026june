FAQ

Questions about the programme.

Is there any fee to join the telecrm affiliate programme?
No. Joining is completely free. There are no setup fees, no monthly charges and no minimum referral requirements to stay active.
Do I need technical knowledge to participate?
Not at all. You don't need to understand the product, explain the features or handle any part of the sales process. All you need is the right contact. We take it from there.
When and how do I receive payments?
Commission payouts are processed on a regular cycle. You'll receive full details on payout schedules and payment methods during onboarding — before you make your first referral.
Can I refer multiple businesses?
Yes — there's no cap on the number of businesses you refer. Every closing referral earns you a 10% recurring commission. The more you refer, the more your passive income compounds over time.
What if I don't get referrals right away?
There's no pressure or penalty for starting slowly. Our team provides onboarding resources and positioning guidance to help you frame telecrm for your specific audience. Most partners find their first referral comes naturally once they understand the product's core value.
