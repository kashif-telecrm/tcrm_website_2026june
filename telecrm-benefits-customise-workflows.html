One builder. Infinite workflows.

From the moment a lead arrives to the day a deal closes — if it happens in your sales process, you can automate it. No two businesses are the same. telecrm workflows match any of them.

Round-robin, weighted or rule-based — you decide how assignment works.

Common questions

Let's clear a few things up.

If yours isn't here, ask us on the demo call.

Do I need to know how to code to build workflows?
telecrm's workflow builder is entirely visual — pick from a list of triggers, connect conditions using dropdowns, and add actions. Most teams build and publish their first workflow within 30 minutes of setup. Our team also builds the first workflow with you during onboarding.
Can workflows send WhatsApp messages automatically?
Yes. You can trigger a WhatsApp message at any point in a workflow — on lead capture, after a stage change, after a missed call, after a delay, or when a payment is confirmed. Messages can include the lead's name, city, product interest or any other field from the lead page, making them feel personal even when fully automated.
Can one workflow handle different conditions for different lead types?
Yes. Each workflow can have multiple "Check If Lead" nodes that branch into different paths. A single workflow can handle Facebook leads, Google leads and IndiaMart leads differently — assigning each to the right team, sending different WhatsApp templates and setting different follow-up timelines. One workflow can replace what previously took six separate manual processes.
What happens if I need to change a workflow after it's published?
You can pause a workflow, edit it and republish without rebuilding from scratch. Changes take effect immediately on the next qualifying lead. You can also duplicate existing workflows and test variations before making them live — so you're never experimenting on your active pipeline.
Does telecrm work for teams in real estate, education and BFSI specifically?
Yes. telecrm is used by sales teams across real estate developers, EdTech platforms, coaching institutes, insurance companies, lending firms and more. The workflow builder adapts to any sales process — there are no fixed templates. Our team helps you configure your first workflows during onboarding, based on your specific process.
