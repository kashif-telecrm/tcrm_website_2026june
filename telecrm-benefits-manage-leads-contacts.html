The problem worth fixing

Most teams don't lose leads.
They misplace them.

When leads come in from six different platforms and get tracked in spreadsheets, things go missing. Not because anyone is careless. Because the system is. Here's what that looks like in practice.

01
Six tabs open just to see today's leads
Facebook in one tab. IndiaMart in another. WhatsApp on a phone you can't see from your desk. Half the morning is gone before anyone has made a call.
02
Monday's hot lead is still sitting on Friday
Nobody picked it up. Nobody knew they were meant to. By the weekend it's gone cold and another competitor has called them three times.
03
The same person, four times, four reps
They enquired on Facebook, then called the office, then filled the website form. Now four people are calling them and you're paying for the same lead four times over.
04
"Show me what we haven't called this week"
Your manager asks. The answer should take ten seconds. In a spreadsheet, it takes ten minutes and probably never makes it back.

Common questions

Things people ask
before getting started.

If you have a question that isn't here, just ask us on the demo call.

What is lead management in a CRM?
Lead management is how a CRM captures every enquiry from every source, sends each one to the right sales rep, tracks its journey through your pipeline and keeps the contact data clean. A good CRM does all of this automatically, so your team spends less time on admin and more time on calls. telecrm handles every part of this in one place.
How does telecrm capture leads from Facebook, IndiaMart and JustDial?
telecrm has native integrations with Facebook Lead Ads, Instagram, IndiaMart, JustDial, TradeIndia, 99Acres, MagicBricks, Housing.com and 14 other platforms. Once you connect them, every new enquiry appears in your telecrm inbox in real time. No manual exports. No copy-paste. No morning download routine.
Can telecrm assign leads to sales reps automatically?
Yes. You set the rules once — by city, language, source, product, team or any custom field — and telecrm assigns every new lead to the right rep within seconds. It supports round-robin, weighted distribution and sticky-agent routing, where repeat enquiries always go back to the same rep.
How does telecrm handle duplicate leads?
telecrm checks every new lead against your existing database in real time. It matches on phone number, email and similar name patterns. When it finds a duplicate, it follows the rule you've set — merge, link, update or skip — so your database stays one clean list and your team never calls the same person twice.
Is telecrm built for Indian sales teams?
Yes. telecrm is designed for the way Indian businesses actually sell — with deep integrations into IndiaMart, JustDial, 99Acres and TradeIndia, full WhatsApp Business API support, regional language handling and pricing built for Indian SMBs. 5,000+ Indian teams use telecrm today, including Mahindra, Domino's, BYJU'S and Housing.com.
How long does it take to set up?
Most teams are live within 48 hours. Our team handles the integrations, imports your existing contact data, sets up your auto-assignment rules and trains your reps. You don't lift a finger beyond pointing us at your lead sources.
