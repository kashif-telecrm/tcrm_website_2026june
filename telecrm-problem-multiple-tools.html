Problems Hub · Multiple Tools

Your team spends an hour a day
in apps, not on calls.

Calling tool. WhatsApp Business. CRM. Excel. Calendar. Email marketing. Five subscriptions, five logins, five places the lead data hides. Here's where the disconnected stack costs you — and how telecrm collapses it into one screen.

Where it breaks down

Six problems your team
is probably living with right now.

Problem 01
Leads arrive in 5 portals that don't talk
Facebook Lead Ads in one inbox. IndiaMart in a second login. JustDial in a third. WhatsApp Business in a fourth. Your website form in a fifth. Every morning, someone manually consolidates five portals into one list — and leads leak at every step.
Problem 02
Calls and WhatsApp live in separate apps
The calling system is one app. WhatsApp Business is another. A rep talking to a single lead switches tabs every two minutes — and loses the thread between what was said on the call and what was sent in the chat.
Problem 03
Conversation history splits across inboxes
WhatsApp thread in one app. Email chain in another. Call notes (if a rep typed any) in a third. The next person to touch the lead sees a third of the context. Every handoff starts with 'can you give me some background?'
Problem 04
Reminders sit in calendars, leads sit in CRM
The CRM holds the lead. Your calendar holds the reminder. The two never know about each other. When the lead's stage changes or the owner shifts, the calendar reminder stays on the old rep's phone — pointing at the wrong lead, the wrong action, the wrong time.
Problem 05
Reports stitched from three spreadsheets
Monthly close report? Export from the calling tool. Conversion by source? Pull from CRM. Average deal size? Manually calculated. Each report takes hours to compile and is out of date before it's presented.
Problem 06
Five subscriptions for what should be one platform
₹2K for calling. ₹3K for WhatsApp API. ₹5K for CRM. ₹2K for email marketing. ₹1K for integration glue. Multiplied by seat licences across a team — ₹50K+ per month for a stack that still doesn't share data between apps.
How telecrm fixes it

Six fixes — one platform.

Every capability below is available on mobile and desktop, configured for your team in hours, and running the moment you go live.

One inbox for every lead source
Every source — Facebook, IndiaMart, JustDial, Google Ads, WhatsApp, your website, custom webhooks — feeds one inbox. No logging into five places, no morning consolidation, no leads lost between portals.
Calls and WhatsApp on the same lead card
Make a call from the lead card. Send a WhatsApp from the same card. Both are logged automatically on the same timeline. Reps see the full conversation — calls and messages — in one place. No tab switching, no context loss.
Full conversation history on every record
Every call, WhatsApp message, email, note and stage change lands on the lead's timeline automatically. The next rep to touch the lead has everything. No 'can you give me some background?' — the background is already there.
Reminders inside the CRM, not in a calendar
Set a follow-up from the lead card. It appears in the rep's daily follow-up list, fires a push notification at the right time and disappears from the queue when done. No calendar app, no sticky note, no mental load.
Reports that build themselves
Conversion by source, calls by rep, pipeline by stage, revenue by campaign — all calculated automatically as your team works. No exporting, no stitching, no formulas. The report is ready before anyone asks for it.
One subscription instead of five
telecrm replaces your standalone dialer, WhatsApp platform, CRM, reminder tool and reporting layer. One platform, one bill, one login, one support contact. Most teams reduce their monthly stack cost by 60% in the first month.
