Before you request a demo

Questions we always get.

Which AI tools does telecrm MCP connect with?
telecrm MCP currently connects with Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini and Grok. MCP — Model Context Protocol — is a standardised connection layer, so as more AI tools adopt it, telecrm will be compatible with them too. You use whichever AI tool your team is already comfortable with.
Is my sales data safe when connected to an AI tool?
Yes. With telecrm MCP, you define the level of access the AI is supposed to have. You can restrict it to specific data types, pipelines or time ranges. Sensitive information stays protected — the AI only sees what you explicitly allow it to see.
Does the AI Call Analyzer work on every call automatically?
The analysis is triggered by clicking "Analyze call with AI" on any recorded call in the activity history. The recording is processed and the full structured analysis — summary, objections, agent skills, AIDA stage, conversion percentage — is generated and logged on the lead's record automatically. No manual input required from the rep.
How accurate is the AI Lead Intelligence score?
The score is generated from the actual conversation — what was said, how objections were handled, what the lead expressed interest in and how far they are in their decision process. It reflects the content of that specific call, not a generic model. Reps can also edit or add context after the analysis is generated.
Can managers use MCP to track individual rep performance?
Yes. You can ask questions like "Which reps have the best closure rate this month?" or "Show me weekly activity for Kavya Sharma over the last 30 days." The AI pulls from your live telecrm data and returns structured, accurate answers. No need to build custom reports or export anything.
